# FitVision AI 👗✨
### AI-Powered Virtual Fashion Try-On Platform

**Live Demo:** [vusmank.github.io/fitvision-ai](https://vusmank.github.io/fitvision-ai/)

---

## What is this?

FitVision AI is a virtual fashion platform that lets you see how clothes look on you — without physically wearing them. You simply turn on your camera or upload a photo, click on any outfit, and the AI instantly places it on your image so you can see how it looks before buying.

---

## What can it do?

### 👤 Virtual Try-On
- Turn on your camera or upload a photo
- The AI scans your body shape and posture
- Click any outfit and it appears on you in real time

### 🤖 AI Outfit Recommendations
- The AI suggests outfits based on your body type, the weather, current fashion trends, and the occasion
- Every outfit gets an **AI Style Score** rating its fit quality, trend match, and overall style

### 🎨 Outfit Customization
- Change colors, patterns, and fabric types
- Choose sleeve styles (short, long, sleeveless)
- Add custom text or logo placement (chest, back, sleeve)
- All changes preview instantly

### 🧩 Mix & Match Engine
- Pick tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories separately
- Combine them into a complete outfit
- Use **Auto Mix** to let the AI build a full look for you

### 📊 Side-by-Side Comparison
- Compare two outfits next to each other
- See scores for fit, trend, and style for each

### 📤 Share for Feedback
- Share your outfit preview with friends via WhatsApp, Instagram, or Email
- Get opinions before you buy

### 🛒 Buy Now
- Single-click checkout
- Smart bundle offers — buy a complete look and save 20%

### 🏭 For Brands & Manufacturers
- Upload clothing designs for digital prototyping
- Preview designs on virtual models before production
- Send designs for customer approval without making physical samples
- Reduces manufacturing waste and return rates

## How to run locally

1. Download or clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in any web browser
3. No installation or server needed — it works directly from your computer

---

## Built with

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies
- Font Awesome icons
- Browser's built-in camera API for live try-on
